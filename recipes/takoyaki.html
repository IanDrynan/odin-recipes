Takoyaki

Description

Japanese dumplings filled with a savory filling

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • bonito flakes
  • soy sauce
  • mirin
  • sesame oil
  • onion
  • octopus

Steps

  1. Boil octopus
  2. Make batter
  3. Use takoyaki skillet and fill half of each space with the batter
  4. Put one piece of octopus in each space
  5. Allow to cook for a couple minutes
  6. Flip each half ball of takoyaki 90 degrees up, so that it exposes half of the bottom of each space.
  7. Fill each space with more batter
  8. Continue to flip until every side is cooked
  9. Serve
